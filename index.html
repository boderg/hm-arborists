The Tree Care Specialists

Pruning & Lopping

We thoroughly assess the tree and remove only the necessary boughs, branches and deadwood to leave your tree with the desired look and shape.
We leave a healthy amount of growth on the tree so the tree can still grow and flourish.

Maintenance

We undertake general maintenance jobs keeping your trees and bushes in a tip top condition.
We use battery powered tools for the environment and to keep the noise down.

Felling

We remove and fell trees that are diseased or causing structural damage.
We avoid the removal (where possible) of all healthy trees.

Stump Removal

As part of the felling service we will remove the stump if requested.
Due to the nature of some trees, a certain amount of root structure may be left in the ground, however we will treat as much as we can so no regrowth appears.
All treatments used are ecologically safe and non toxic.
